
infinite Theme v3.1

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The infinite Theme is a WordPress theme that has no style applied to it, nor any extra mark-up that doesn't directly support the content or functionality of the the theme.

The infinite Theme is the perfect place to start for creating new themes.
